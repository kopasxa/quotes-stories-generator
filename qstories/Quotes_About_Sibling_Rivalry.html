Quotes About Sibling Rivalry

1. “That’s the best part of having siblings. Half the time, you want to kill each other, but you’d kill for each other, too.” – L.C. Davis

2. “A sister is someone, who loves you from the heart. No matter how much you argue, you cannot be drawn apart.” – Shiv Sharma

3. “What brothers say to tease their sisters has nothing to do with what they really think of them.” – Esther M. Friesner

4. “I see a brother and sister, sitting over there together, children fighting over a toy, they just can’t seem to see, their love shining through, with every smile that they bring.” – Valerie Dupont

5. “In many sibling relationships, the rate of conflict can be high, but the fun times in the backyard and the basement more than balance it out.” – Po Bronson and Ashley Merryman

6. “I think people that have a brother or sister don’t realize how lucky they are. Sure, they fight a lot, but to know that there’s always somebody there, somebody that’s family.” – Trey Parker & Matt Stone

7. “Siblings: children of the same parents, each of whom is perfectly normal until they get together.” – Sam Levenson

8. “What causes sibling rivalry? Having more than one kid.” – Tim Allen

9. “There is a little boy inside the man who is my brother… Oh, how I hated that little boy. And how I love him too.” – Anna Quindlen

10. “Siblings that say they never fight are most definitely hiding something.” – Lemony Snicket
