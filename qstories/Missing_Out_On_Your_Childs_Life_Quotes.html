Missing Out On Your Child’s Life Quotes

1. “Why do you go away? So that you can come back. So that you can see the place you came from with new eyes and extra colors. And the people there see you differently, too. Coming back to where you started is not the same as never leaving.” – Terry Pratchett

2. “I miss my kids sometimes and that can get me down when I’ve been away working, but then I wake up and recognize how incredibly lucky I am. Spending time being down is less time out there achieving and enjoying.” – Sean Combs

3. “It’s always nice to have someone in your life who can make you smile even when they’re not around.” – Anonymous

4. “The ache for home lives in all of us. The place where we can go as we are and not be questioned.” – Maya Angelou

5. “The happiest moments of my life have been the few which I have passed at home in the bosom of my family.” – Thomas Jefferson

6. “You may no longer be small, or need my help for all, but you’ll always need love from my heart, especially when we are apart.” – Anonymous

7. “Missing someone is part of loving them. Not until you are apart do you realize how much they mean to you.” – Nikhil Saluja

8. “Where we love is home, home that our feet may leave, but not our hearts.” – Oliver Wendell Holmes

9. “We may be miles apart, but our love and affection unites us.” – Anonymous

10. “Even though my lap is too small, my heart will always have enough room for you.” – Anonymous

11. “Maybe you had to leave in order to really miss a place; maybe you had to travel to figure out how beloved your starting point was.” – Jodi Picoult

12. “My love for you is like an invisible elastic band. No matter how far you go, we are connected.” – Anonymous

13. “I know you must walk your own path, but let my love be the light to guide you.” – Anonymous

14. “I would really like to spend more time with the family. Every time I go abroad I miss them all dreadfully.” – Jilly Cooper

15. “Homesickness is not always a vague, nostalgic, almost beautiful emotion, although that is somehow the way we always seem to picture it in our mind. It can be a terribly keen blade…It can change the way one looks at the world.” – Stephen King (The Body)

16. “As a family, we make – not take – time for each other. That is the key to a strong chain even though some links don’t touch.” – Anonymous

17. “How lucky I am to have something that makes saying goodbye so hard.” – A.A. Milne (Winnie-the-Pooh)

18. “Home is wherever you leave everything you love and never question that it will be there when you return.” – Leo Christopher

19. “You leave home to seek your fortune and, when you get it, you go home and share it with your family.” – Anita Baker

20. “Missing you comes in waves, tonight I’m drowning.” – Anonymous

21. “I said to myself, ‘I’ve waited a long time in my life to have a child, and I’m missing it, I want to continue to have a career, but not this way.’” – Karen Allen

22. “I recognize the amount of time that it takes as a director. I made the choice to stop taking roles outside of L.A. because I didn’t want to miss any of my child’s life.” – Regina King

23. “Missing someone isn’t about how long it has been since you’ve seen them or the amount of time since you’ve talked. It’s about that very moment when you’re doing something and wishing they were right there with you.” – Anonymous

24. “Be a part of my kid’s life or not. I won’t beg you. You’re missing out, not them. Just don’t get mad when they don’t know who you are.” – Anonymous

25. “Distance gives us a reason to love harder.” – Anonymous

26. “Days were better when we were together.” – Anonymous
