10. “Mom, we want you to know that we really love you and miss your presence even though you are no longer with us. In heaven, we want you to be happy and wish you a happy birthday.”

15. “There are no words, dearest Mom, that can fully express how much I miss you today. You will always be the most valuable part of my life. Happy birthday, mamma.”

17. “You have enriched my life, dear Mom, and made me who I am today. I miss you so much, particularly today on your birthday. With all the angels, I want you to enjoy your birthday in heaven. I love you a lot.”

18. “Mom, today you may be gone, but in my heart, your love and memories are forever saved. Enjoy your heavenly special day, dear mom!”

19. “Have a beautiful birthday, mom. When I woke up this morning, you were the first person I thought of. I remembered that it was your birthday today, but I’m so sad you’re not with us anymore. I just hope you’re happy, wherever you might be.”

20. “Although it’s painful, we continue to celebrate your birthday every year. We all work hard to make a great birthday party for everyone who misses you. Have a happy birthday, mom.”

21. “You’re always loved, forever missed. On this day, I want to send a lot of hugs and kisses up to Heaven for you. Happy birthday, mom.”

22. “Mom, the day that you passed away was the saddest day of my life. I know that you are comfortable and free from all life’s troubles wherever you are, but I still miss you a lot. I know that one day in heaven, I will be with you. Happy birthday.”

23. “Dearest mother, you’ll always be missed. Every time I think of the good times we had, a smile and a tear appear on my face. Mom, I’m always going to miss you. Happy birthday in heaven.”
