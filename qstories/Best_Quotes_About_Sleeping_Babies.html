Best Quotes About Sleeping Babies

1. “Having my baby fall asleep in my arms takes away all of my worries and stresses. A sense of complete and total peace comes over me.” – Maria Jose Ovalle

2. “I am your mother. You are my child. I am your quiet place. You are my wild. I am your calm face. You are my giggle. I am your wait. You are my wiggle. I am your dinner. You are my chocolate cake. I am your bedtime. You are my wide awake. I am your lullaby. You are my peek-a-boo. I am your goodnight kiss. You are my I love you.” – Unknown

3. “A day without a nap for a baby is like a cupcake without frosting.” – Unknown

4. “Let them sleep, for when they wake they will move mountains.” – Unknown

5. “The babies are amazing… They begin each day all warm and sleepy, smelling of promise.” – Julia Roberts

6. “There was never a child so lovely but his mother was glad to get him to sleep.” – Ralph Waldo Emerson

7. “Little boys should never be put to sleep. They always wake up a day older.” – Peter Pan

8. “A sleeping baby is really good at doing the one thing you don’t get to do anymore.” – Linda Poindexter

9. “My aunt gave me the best advice: Put her to sleep yourself every night. Sing to her and cradle her in your arms and sit by her side – every night. Because one day you won’t be able to.” – Salma Hayek

10. “Cleaning and scrubbing can wait till tomorrow, for babies grow up, we’ve learned to our sorrow, so quiet down, cobwebs. Dust, go to sleep. I’m rocking my baby, and babies don’t keep.” – Unknown

11. “A sleeping baby is a little slice of heaven.” – Unknown.

12. “And he thinks that if his soul had a form, this is what it would be. A baby sleeping in his arms.” – Neal Shusterman

13. “There are hard days in motherhood. But looking at your sleeping baby reminds you why it’s all worth it.” – Kara Ferwerda

14. “I know I should sleep when she is sleeping, but I can’t take my eyes off her.” – Unknown

15. “There is no joy like that of your baby falling asleep on you.” – Unknown

16. “He stole my sleep, stole my bed, but also my heart.” – Unknown

17. “My heart feels like it is going to burst when I watch my baby sleep.” – Unknown

18. “I’m pretty confident baby dreams fill all the magic in the world.” – Unknown

19. “Kiss your children goodnight, even if they are already asleep.” – H. Jackson Brown, Jr.
