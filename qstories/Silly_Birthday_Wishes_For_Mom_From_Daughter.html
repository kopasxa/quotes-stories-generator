Silly Birthday Wishes For Mom From Daughter

1. “It’s amazing how good you look for your age, considering all the grief I gave you when I was younger. Happy Birthday, Mom!”

2. “Hey Mom, this is your special day, so we are sending dad away on an extended vacation. Wish you a lovely birthday!”

3. “All of my crazy comes from you, and I wouldn’t have it any other way. Happy Birthday, Mom!”

4. “I knew exactly what you wanted for your birthday this year. Me… out of the house for a day. Happy Birthday, Mom!”

5. “Keep calm! It’s my mother’s birthday. However, she doesn’t keep calm when things don’t go her way. Happy birthday to my strong-willed and sweet mom.”

6. “Mom, a great birthday is the least we can do for you after all you’ve put up with over the years. Happy Birthday from your obnoxious kids.”

7. “Mom you’re the greatest, Mom you’re the best, you put up with all of us, even though we’re real pests! You feed us, you help us, and you’re always there, so we’re giving you this birthday card, just to be fair!”

8. “Happy Birthday Mom! Now that I’m older, I want to say thank you for never leaving me in a shopping cart and running away. I know it was tempting, and no one would have blamed you.”

9. “Hey mother, you have hit the age where gifts, chocolates, and blessings don’t make any sense, but a wig and wrinkle removal cream surely do! Jokes aside, happy birthday to my super amazing mom.”

10. “You are such a great mother. I’ve always wondered how you put up with me all these years and kept your cool. Then, I found your wine stash and things became clear to me! Happy Birthday, let’s pour a glass and celebrate!”

11. “You are my favorite parent as long as I am your favorite kid and I get my bribe on time! Happy birthday to you my dear mother!”

12. “Happy Xth anniversary of your 25th bday to my favorite mom! Enjoy your day!”

13. “9 out of 10 scientists agree: The more birthdays you have, the longer you live. Happy Birthday, Mom!”

14. “Everything about you is perfect, but I hate it when you snatch all the money our relatives give to me. Happy birthday to my super funny and stubborn mother!”

15. “Mothers and daughters are like two peas in a pod, two kittens in a basket, two peaches in a tree. Happy birthday to my mother and best friend.”

16. “I’m so lucky to have a mom who still parties like she’s in her twenties. Happy Birthday and enjoy your special day!”

17. “It’s time to crank up the music, pour out the drinks, and let the good times begin! Happy birthday to a very special mom. You make every day delightful.”

18. “Hey Mom, thank goodness that I’m finally old enough to show you how I really party. Happy Birthday to you!”

19. “Happy Birthday, Mom! I’m so happy that you look so good, as I got to inherit those good-looking genes.”
