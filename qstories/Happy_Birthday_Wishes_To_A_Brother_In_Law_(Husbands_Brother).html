12. “A very cool birthday to the coolest brother-in-law. You are the reason I am comfortable in your house, apart from your brother. You make me feel I am your own sister. Live long and stay happy!”

13. “Happy birthday to my brother-in-law who just so happens to have the world’s best sister-in-law! Have an awesome day today, bro!”

14. “I always thought it would take time to be comfortable with you as I came to this house later, but your joyous mood, sense of humor, and love made me instantly feel like a part of the family. Happy birthday, brother. May God bless you!”

15. “A very warm wish to my only brother-in-law on your birthday. I hope you remain as calm, courageous, and beautiful as you always have been. Happy birthday!”

16. “Happy birthday to the best-extended brother. May you have a joyful, merry, and simply amazing birthday!”

17. “Only a lucky girl like me gets to share a wonderful bond with her brother-in-law. We are not only relatives, but also good friends. Happy birthday to my dear brother-in-law.”

18. “I always thought my life would be different once I got married. But, having a cool brother at home helped me be my usual self. Happy Birthday to the coolest brother-in-law in the world!”
