Short Happy Birthday Messages For Mom In Heaven

1. “I wish Heaven didn’t have to take you so soon, mama. Happy birthday, I love you.”

2. “Mom, memories of you are my life’s only solace. I wish you were here. Happy birthday.”

3. “Happy birthday to the strongest woman I’ve ever known. You’re no longer with me but I feel your love every day. Happy birthday.”

4. “There is not a single day in my life that does not remind me of you, my mother, my best friend, and now my angel. Happy birthday.”

5. “On this day, we remember your love, your light, and all you did for us. Have a wonderful birthday in Heaven, mom.”

6. “Children never outgrow the love of their mother, no matter how old they get. Happy birthday to a lovely mom who I’ll never forget.”

7. “For so many years you were my guide. Now you’re an angel always by my side. Happy birthday, mom.”

8. “On your special day, I smile as I remember your kindness, class, and unconditional love. Please know that you’re never far from my heart. Happy birthday, mom.”

9. “Life goes on without you here but wonderful memories of you will never fade. Today and always, we remember the difference you made. Happy birthday, mom.”

10. “Throughout the years, I may have put up a fight. But, now I understand that you were my guiding light. Happy birthday forever, mom.”

11. “Each day I miss you more but one thing is always sure. Your love remains and will always stay. I wish you a peaceful resting place and a wonderful afterlife. Happy birthday.”

12. “Today and always, one thing rings true. I’m forever grateful to have had a mom such as you. Happy birthday in Heaven.”

13. “Mom, thanks for being there through thick and thin. I look forward to the day we’ll meet again. Happy birthday.”

14. “There’s something about a mother that is so special. Happy birthday to a mom who will always be cherished and loved.”

15. “It doesn’t matter if we’re near or far. I’ll send birthday wishes to you on a shooting star. Have a wonderful day in Heaven, mom.”

16. “Happy birthday, mommy. You were my first thought this morning, and I wish you nothing but peace on your special day today.”

17. “Mom, you taught me that there’s nothing I can’t do. So today I’m sending birthday wishes up to Heaven for you!”

18. “To our mother in heaven, we will miss you on your birthdays years from now just as much as on this birthday. Happy birthday.”

19. “A beautiful flower never loses its beauty and value even when it is gone. Happy birthday, mom.”

20. “We may be far apart in distance, but you’re always close to us at heart. Happy birthday my dearest mother.”

21. “How are you, mommy? It’s your special day again this year. We still miss you so much and we wish you a blessed birthday from Earth.”

22. “Nobody will ever be able to replace you in our entire lifetime. Happy birthday, mommy.”

23. “You have been nothing but the best mom and best friend to us all on earth. You are dearly missed, and we wish you a happy birthday.”

24. “Mom, did you receive all of our past birthday wishes when we visited your final resting place? Happy birthday again, mommy. We love you.”

25. “Words cannot describe how much we miss your presence with us. Our memories of you will live on forever.”

26. “If I could make a wish, I would wish to celebrate this special day just once more with you. Happy birthday, momma.”
