​Beautiful Family Quotes

1. “A happy family is but an earlier heaven.” – George Bernard Shaw

2. “Our most basic instinct is not for survival but for family.” – Paul Pearsall

3. “A mother discovers that one does not love one’s children just because they are one’s children but of the friendship formed while raising them.” – Gabriel Garcia Marquez

4. “In every conceivable manner, the family is a link to our past, the bridge to our future.” – Alex Haley

5. “I sustain myself with the love of family.” – Maya Angelou

6. “You deserve the best I have to give, and that’s my undying love as long as you shall live.” – Anonymous

7. “People come and go in our lives, but the beauty of life is that our family is always there.” – Catherine Pulsifer

8. “Getting together as a family is like putting all the chapters in a book, you need each member for the family to make sense.” – Anonymous

9. “Close together or far apart, you’re forever in my heart.” – Anonymous

10. “That’s what people who love you do. They put their arms around you and love you when you’re not so lovable.” – Deb Caletti

11. “Other things may change us, but we start and end with family.” – Anthony Brandt

12. “Having a place to go – is a home. Having someone to love – is a family. Having both – is a blessing.” – Donna Hedges

13. “Families are the compasses that guide us. They are the inspiration to reach great heights, and our comfort when we occasionally falter.” – Brad Henry

14. “Your family love must be cultivated. Time, effort, and imagination must be summoned to keep any relationship flourishing and growing.” – Jim Rohn

15. “The time you spend with family keeps your inner clock running.” – Anonymous

16. “This is part of what a family is about; knowing that they will be there watching out for you. Nothing else will give you that.” – Mitch Albom

17. “Home is people. Not a place. If you go back there after the people are gone, then all you can see is what is not there anymore.” – Robin Hobb

18. “Family is the most important thing in the world.” – Princess Diana

19. “The family is a haven in a heartless world.” – Christopher Lasch

20. “Ohana means family – no one gets left behind, and no one is ever forgotten.” – Chris Sanders & Dean DeBlois (Lilo & Stitch)

21. “Family is not an important thing. It’s everything.” – Michael J. Fox

22. “I do think that families are the most beautiful things in all the world!” – Louisa May Alcott

23. “The strength of a family, like the strength of an army, is in its loyalty to each other.” – Mario Puzo

24. “Home is a place you grow up wanting to leave, and grow old wanting to get back to.” – John Ed Pearce

25. “Family traditions counter alienation and confusion. They help us define who we are; they provide something steady, reliable, and safe in a confusing world.” – Susan Lieberman
