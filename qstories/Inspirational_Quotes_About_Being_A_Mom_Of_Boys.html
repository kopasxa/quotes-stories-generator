Inspirational Quotes About Being A Mom Of Boys

1. “Mother is her son’s first god. She must teach him the most important lesson of all – how to love.” – T. F. Hodge

2. “And she loved a little boy very, very much – even more than she loved herself.” – Shel Silverstein

3. “There has never been, nor will there ever be, anything quite so special as the love between the mother and a son.” – Unknown

4. “Mothers are inscrutable beings to their sons, always.” – A.E. Coppard

5. “Happy is the son whose faith in his mother remains unchallenged.” – Louisa May Alcott

6. “My mom was the person that I most looked up to for her point of view, her humor.” – Ben Stiller

7. “A man never sees all that his mother has been to him until it’s too late to let her know that he sees it.” – W. D. Howells

8. “There are no words or boy mom quotes that can express my ultimate love for you.” – Unknown

9. “‘My mother is the reason that I love you,’ Bhim said simply. ‘She is the reason I know what love is.’” – Leah Franqui

10. “You will be his first kiss, his first love, his first friend. You’re his momma and he is your whole world. He is your little boy.” – Unknown

11. “To a mother, a son is never a fully grown man; and a son is never a fully grown man until he understands and accepts this about his mother.” – Unknown

12. “But boys are different from girls and not just physically. Acknowledging, appreciating, and understanding that boys are boys is essential in raising a son.” – Catherine Musco Garcia-Prats

13. “It’s one thing for a child to leave home and live close by but, to me, it is quite another for my son to leave to live in another province where distance prevents me from seeing him more often. Then, I realize that the values and the teachings I have given him over the years are more important than any words that I would say now.” – Catherine Pulsifer

14. “When you teach your son, you teach your son’s son.” – The Talmud

15. “It was vitally important to me over the years of my son’s development that I modeled the behavior that I wanted him to adopt. You see, as a parent or as parents, we all have a tremendous responsibility that should not be taken lightly.” – Catherine Pulsifer

16. “Bringing up a daughter, a mother pours a part of her soul. Bringing up a son, she gives him her whole soul.” – Unknown

17. “Let your boys test their wings. They may not be eagles, but that doesn’t mean they shouldn’t soar free.” – C.J. Milbrandt

18. “A mother is the only woman who is able to understand your tears without judging you.” – Unknown

19. “God could not be everywhere, and therefore he made mothers.” – Rudyard Kipling

20. “No matter how strong, independent, or ‘man’ you are, the first thing that comes to your mind when you’re sick is, ‘I wish Mom was here.’” – Unknown

21. “I want my son to become aware that he is in charge of the choices he makes, and it’s good to make thoughtful good choices.” – Karen Salmansohn

22. “Even more than the time when she gave birth, a mother feels her greatest joy when she hears others refer to her son as a wise learned one.” – Thiruvalluvar

24. “A mother understands what a child does not say.” – Jewish Proverb

25. “I cannot be more grateful for the things that I have because I love my son and I have him.” – Unknown

26. “At first blush, everyone thinks the most important relationships in the family are the father-son and mother-daughter relationships. But they’re wrong. The cross-gender relationships are most critical.” – Dr. Kevin Leman

27. “A dad for his son is always caring, while mommy is always loving.” – Unknown

28. “A son is the only man who can complete his mother’s womanhood.” – Unknown
