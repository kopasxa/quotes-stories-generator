My Favorite Brother And Sister Quotes

1. “We didn’t even realize we were making memories, we just knew we were having fun.” – Winnie the Pooh

2. “Side by side or miles apart, we’re brother and sister connected by the heart.” – Unknown

3. “As brother and sister our paths shall change, but the special bond between us will last forever.” – Unknown

4. “I have a wonderful shelter, which is my family. I have a wonderful relationship with my brother and sister; this makes me feel that I know always where I belong.” – Jose Carreras

5. “Brothers and sisters can provide the most encouragement and support when life’s trials get us down. Talk to them!” – Catherine Pulsifer

6. “I think people that have a brother or sister don’t realize how lucky they are. Sure, they fight a lot, but to know that there’s always somebody there, somebody that’s family.” – Trey Parker and Matt Stone

7. “It leaves a smile on my face when I think of those trifling fights we used to have and then suddenly made up. The memories may fade away with passing time but the love we share will only grow.” – Unknown

8. “A sibling is the lens through which you see your childhood.” – Ann Hood

9. “The bond that binds us is beyond choice. We are brothers. We are brothers in what we share.” – Ursula K. Le Guin

10. “A trial between brothers has no winners or losers.” – Ekonda Proverb

11. “Blessed is the servant who loves his brother as much as when he is sick and useless as when he is well and can be of service to him. And blessed is he who loves his brother as well when he is afar off as when he is by his side, and who would say nothing behind his back he might not, in love, say before his face.” – St. Francis of Assisi
