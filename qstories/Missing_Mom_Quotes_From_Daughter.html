Missing Mom Quotes From Daughter

1. “I loved my mother too,’ I said. ‘I still do. That’s the thing it never goes away, even if the person does.” – Anna Carey

2. “I know everyone calls heaven so beautiful – because it has my mother. I miss you, mom.” – Unknown.

3. “Mom, I know you are listening from above. There’s nothing that I value more than your love. No matter where I am or what I’m doing, your memories will always keep me smiling.” – Unknown

4. “Mother was comfort. Mother was home. A girl who lost her mother was suddenly a tiny boat on an angry ocean. Some boats eventually floated ashore. And some boats, like me, seemed to float farther and farther from land.” – Ruta Sepetys

5. “Whenever I am missing you, I also remember how fortunate I was that you were in my life. I wouldn’t trade those moments for the world.” – Cindy Adkins

6. “A daughter without her mother is a woman broken. It is a loss that turns to arthritis and settles deep into her bones.” – Kristin Hannah

7. “I truly never learned what the words ‘I miss you’ were until I reached for my mom’s hand and it wasn’t there.” – Unknown

8. “When someone asks you where you come from, the answer is your mother…When your mother’s gone, you’ve lost your past. It’s so much more than love. Even when there’s no love, it’s so much more than anything else in your life. I did love my mother, but I didn’t know how much until she was gone.” – Anna Quindlen

9. “I have an angel watching over me. She is my mom.” – Unknown

10. “I miss the warmth of your gentle hug and the love I felt when my arms were wrapped around you. I miss seeing your beautiful smile and the sound of your voice saying my name. I miss hearing you say, ‘I love you,’ and me saying, ‘I love you’ in return.” – Millie P. Lorenz

11. “Mother, you left us beautiful memories, your love is still our guide, although we cannot see you, you’re always at our side.” – Unknown

12. “Maybe all over the world there were daughters who knew their mothers as young girls and old women, inside and out, deep. I wasn’t one of them. Even when I was a baby, my memory of her is being only halfway here.” – Jacqueline Woodson

13. “Loss of a mother can never be replaced, but the love of a mother can never be lost.” – Kelly Flannery

14. “No mother and daughter ever live apart, no matter what the distance between them.” — Christie Watson
