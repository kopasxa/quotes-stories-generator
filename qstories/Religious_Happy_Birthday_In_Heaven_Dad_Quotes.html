Religious Happy Birthday In Heaven Dad Quotes

1. “What Heaven wishes Heaven gets, for some reason it wanted you. God was probably missing an angel. We have to make peace with His wishes even though it is hard. We wish you a happy birthday daddy. We miss you.”

2. “Thank you oh gracious Lord – you have allowed my Dad to take care of us, and to love us during his stay on earth. Please take care of my Dad now in heaven for all of eternity, because he deserves so much love always. Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

3. “Happy birthday my father in heaven. I always thought you were special, and it seems God thought so too, as he took you to heaven. Happy heavenly birthday Dad, you are forever in our hearts.”

4. “Happy birthday, to my loving Dad in heaven. Enjoy the peace that only comes in heaven. I remember your struggles with this earthly place and everything you had to live with. How brave and strong you were to endure it all, and I know that I too have to carry on. Peace has found you, and one day I will join you. May the divine peace heal all that the world did to you. You are my hero, Dad.”

5. “Happy birthday in heaven, Dad. There’s no greater experience than to be with the Lord, yet still, I feel compelled to send you my love. I know that you lack nothing, but I still have the need to give you my love. My human heart aches to be close to you, and so today, on your birthday, I am celebrating your life.”

6. “Dearest Dad in heaven, happy birthday. To be in the presence of the Divine must be a dream come true for you – I am so happy for you. I can just imagine you saying, ‘Now I have woken up!’ I know you are in the place you want to be. I look forward to the day when I can finally join you.”

7. “So thankful God blessed me with a wonderful dad. Even though our time together couldn’t last forever, I’ll carry you in my heart forever. Happy birthday in heaven.”

8. “Happy Birthday in heaven, Dad. Certainly, good things come to those who wait. And you have waited… now God takes you in His arms and celebrates your birthday with you. I wait for the day that I can be in your arms again, and I wait for the day that I can say happy birthday to you again, face to face.”

9. “God blessed me with a caring and wonderful father. Even though you’re no longer here on earth, I can still feel your love and pride for me, and look back at our memories together and smile. Happy birthday in heaven.”

10. “Wishing a very happy birthday in heaven to the world’s greatest dad. Every day I try to see the world as you taught me, seeing God in myself and in those around me. This simple thing has given me so much joy, and is your lasting gift to me. On your birthday, I wish I could give you a gift, but it makes me happy to know you are now beyond the need or want for anything this earth can offer. Congratulations, dad, you have won the eternal prize.”

11. “If I had my way, I would still have you in my presence. But luckily for you, God has His way, and He always had a plan to have you safely in His light and love in heaven. Thank you for spending some time with me in life – I hold onto that time until I see you again. Happy birthday, Dad.”

12. “Dad, is it true there are no wishes in heaven because everything is granted? That’s just where I hope to be, but I will wait for that day when you can tell me all about it! Happy birthday, Dad.”

13. “Dad, though it pains me to feel your absence from my earthly life, it gives me great comfort to know that you are one with the Lord. Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

14. “Sending my warmest wishes to you, Dad, on your birthday. Heaven gained another angel the day that you left us. You will forever be remembered and loved by all who knew you. I will celebrate your memory today.”

15. “How sweet it must be to celebrate your birthday in the heavenly realm. I am thinking of everything about you, and I wish I was there! Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

16. “The Lord has called you to enjoy heavenly glorious things. I wait for the day to join you in these glorious celebrations, but for now, from earth, I send you all my love, and I pray that you feel it. Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

17. “Dad, you’ve looked after me very well. I wish you could still hold my hand. But I imagine our heavenly Father holding your hand today on your birthday. Enjoy the peace that this world could not give you, my wonderful Dad. You deserve heavenly bliss. Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

18. “In heaven, that’s where you surely are. Happy Birthday, my loving Dad. Thank you, dear God, for giving me my Dad. We enjoyed the togetherness you’ve given us, and we wait for the day we will be reunited.”

19. “Happy heavenly birthday to my loving Dad. To always see the bigger picture by valuing life more than material things – that’s what I think of when I think of you. I hope you are celebrating with the angels today.”

20. “I know you are in heaven, where angels belong. You were always an angel to me, but I never thought the day would come when I wouldn’t be able to see you on your birthday. Please hear my message, and please send me yours. I need you still. Happy birthday.”

21. “On earth, you had earthly perfection. Now you have heavenly perfection. I want you here, but you are an angel and angels belong in a better world. Happy birthday, Dad.”

22. “May God bless your loving soul forever. I want to touch your face, and I want to hold your hand, but somehow I know that you have never been quite as near to me as you are right now. I feel you everywhere today, and I know you hear me! Happy birthday, Dad.”
