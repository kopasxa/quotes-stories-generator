Sweet Sister Quotes

1. “Sweet is the voice of a sister in the season of sorrow.” – Benjamin Disraeli

2. “For there is no friend like a sister in calm or stormy weather; To cheer one on the tedious way, to fetch one if one goes astray, to lift one if one totters down, to strengthen whilst one stands.” – Christina Rosetti

3. “When traveling life’s journey it’s good to have a sister’s hand to hold on to.” – Unknown

4. “As I grew up, one of my strongest allies has been my sister.” – Patti Smith

5. “If you want to know how your girl will treat you after marriage, just listen to her talking to her little brother.” – Sam Levenson

6. “Sisters are angels who lift us to our feet when our wings have trouble remembering how to fly.” – Unknown

7. “What sets sisters apart from brothers and also from friends is a very intimate mashing of heart, soul, and the mystical cords of memory.” – Carol Saline

8. “Is solace anywhere more comforting than that in the arms of a sister?” – Alice Walker

9. “A sister is a gift to the heart. A friend to the spirit. A golden thread to the meaning of life.” – Unknown

10. “A sister is both your mirror – and your opposite.” – Elizabeth Fishel

11. “No life’s worth more than any other. No sister is worth less than any brother.” – Michael Franti

12. “The mildest, drowsiest sister has been known to turn tiger if her sibling is in trouble.” – Clara Ortega

13. “Lord help the mister that comes between me and my sister.” – Irving Berlin

14. “Sisters are different flowers from the same garden.” – Unknown

15. “Big sisters are the crab grass in the lawn of life.” – Charles M. Schulz

16. “How do people make it through life without a sister?” – Sara Corpening

17. “A ministering angel shall my sister be.” – William Shakespeare

18. “What’s the good of news if you haven’t a sister to share it?” – James DeVries

19. “The ones that stay with you through everything – they’re your true best friends. Don’t let go of them. Also remember, sisters make the best friends in the world.” – Marilyn Monroe

20. “There’s no better friend than a sister.” – Mary Engelbreit

21. “When life’s hills become too steep to climb on my own, my sister takes my hand and reminds me that I didn’t have to climb it alone to begin with.” – Unknown

22. “In the cookies of life, sisters are the chocolate chips.” – Unknown

23. “When I say I won’t tell anyone, my sister doesn’t count.” – Unknown

24. “In thee my soul shall own combined the sister and the friend.” – Catherine Killigrew

25. “Sisterly love is, of all sentiments, the most abstract. Nature does not grant it any functions.” – Ugo Betti

26. “You can kid the world, but not your sister.” – Charlotte Gray
