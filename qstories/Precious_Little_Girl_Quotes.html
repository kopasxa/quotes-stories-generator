Precious Little Girl Quotes

1. “Sugar and spice and all things nice, that’s what little girls are made of.” – Traditional nursery rhyme

2. “She’s laughter and teardrops, so small and brand new, and amazingly angelic, she’s sent to bless you.” – Unknown

3. “Giggles, curls, ribbons, and bows! Our baby girl is so adorable from head to toe.” – Unknown

4. “The smiling face of every little girl is the signature of God’s presence.” – Amit Ray

5. “A daughter is a bundle of firsts that excite and delight, giggles that come from deep inside and are always contagious, everything wonderful and precious and your love for her knows no bounds.” – Barbara Cage

6. “Angels are often disguised as daughters.” – Unknown

7. “She makes the day brighter. She leaves a little sparkle wherever she goes.” – Kate Spade

8. “Little girls dance their way into your heart, whirling on the tips of angel wings, scattering gold dust and kisses in our paths.” – Unknown

9. “Little girls bring such delight with hearts so warm and smiles so bright!” – Unknown

10. “Grace was in all her steps. Heaven in her eye. In every gesture, dignity and love.” – John Milton

11. “I remember I thought you looked like an angel wrapped in pink so soft and warm. You’ve had me wrapped around your finger since the day you were born.” – Tim McGraw (My Little Girl)

12. “Little girls are precious gifts, wrapped in love serene. Their dresses tied with sashes and futures tied with dreams.” – Unknown

13. “Like stardust glistening on fairies’ wings, little girls dreams are of magical things.” – Sherry Larson

14. “A daughter is one of the most beautiful gifts this world has to give.” – Laurel Atherton

15. “Little girls are the nicest things that happen to people. They are born with a little bit of angel shine about them, and though it wears thin sometimes there is always enough left to lasso your heart.” – Alan Beck

16. “Ten tiny little fingers that always want to play, that never stop exploring the wonder of today. Ten tiny little fingers that from the very start, will reach out for tomorrow yet always hold your heart.” – Unknown

17. “Such a big miracle in such a little girl.” – Unknown

18. “There is a garden in her eyes, where roses and white lilies flow.” – Thomas Campion

19. “A daughter may outgrow your lap, but she’ll never outgrow your heart.” – Unknown

20. “When we asked for a daughter, we were sent a princess.” – Sally Huss

21. “Hold her a little longer, rock her a little more. Read her another story, you’ve only read her four. Let her sleep on your shoulder, rejoice in her happy baby smile. She is only a little girl for such a little while.” – Unknown

22. “Isn’t she lovely, isn’t she wonderful, isn’t she precious, less than one minute old… Isn’t she pretty, truly the angel’s best. Boy, I’m so happy, we have been heaven blessed. I can’t believe what God has done, through us he’s given life to one. But isn’t she lovely, made from love.” – Stevie Wonder (Isn’t She Lovely)

23. “Some girls are just born with glitter in their veins.” – Paris Hilton

24. “Her smile beams like sunshine, which fills our hearts with love.” – Unknown

25. “Get up and stop crying, my little girl. If you don’t have new clothes to wear, don’t worry, still stars are whispering about your beauty.” – M. F. Moonzajer (A Moment With God)

26. “She leaves a little sparkle wherever she goes.” – Unknown

27. “A baby girl is one of the most beautiful miracles in life, one of the greatest joys we can ever know, and one of the reasons why there is a little extra sunshine, laughter, and happiness in your world today.” – Unknown
