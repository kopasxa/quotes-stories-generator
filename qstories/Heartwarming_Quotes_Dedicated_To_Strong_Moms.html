Heartwarming Quotes Dedicated To Strong Moms

1. “Mother is the heartbeat in the home; and without her, there seems to be no heartthrob.” – Leroy Brownlow

2. “Mothers are like glue. Even when you can’t see them, they’re still holding the family together.” – Susan Gale

3. “Mother is the name for God in the lips and hearts of little children.” – William Makepeace Thackeray

4. “There is no way to be the perfect mother, but a million ways to be a good one” – Jill Churchill

5. “We have a secret in our culture, and it’s not that birth is painful. It’s that women are strong.” – Laura Stavoe Harm

6. “Birth takes a woman’s deepest fears about herself and shows her that she is stronger than them.” – Unknown

7. “Sometimes the strength of motherhood is greater than natural laws.” – Barbara Kingsolver

8. “Motherhood is the biggest gamble in the world. It is the glorious life force. It’s huge and scary – it’s an act of infinite optimism.” – Gilda Radner

9. “Any mother could do the jobs of several air traffic controllers with ease.” – Lisa Alther

10. “Mothers and their children are in a category all their own. There’s no bond so strong in the entire world. No love so instantaneous and forgiving.” – Gail Tsukiyama

11. “If evolution really works, how come mothers only have two hands?” – Milton Berle

12. “The influence of a mother in the lives of her children is beyond calculation.” – James E. Faust

13. “It may be possible to gild pure gold, but who can make his mother more beautiful?” – Mahatma Gandhi

14. “To all mothers in every circumstance, including those who struggle, I say, ‘Be peaceful.’ Believe in God and yourself. You are doing better than you think you are.” – Jeffrey R. Holland

15. “I believe the choice to become a mother is the choice to become one of the greatest spiritual teachers there is.” – Oprah Winfrey

16. “Whatever else is unsure in this stinking dunghill of a world, a mother’s love is not.” – James Joyce

17. “There is no role in life that is more essential than that of motherhood.” – Elder M. Russell Ballard

18. “Youth fades; love droops; the leaves of friendship fall; A mother’s secret hope outlives them all.” – Oliver Wendell Holmes

19. “Being a mother is learning about strengths you didn’t know you had, and dealing with fears you didn’t know existed.” – Linda Wooten

20. “To be a mother you must be strong. Even if you don’t feel it, you have to pretend.” – Sade Adu

21. “Women have always been the strong ones of the world. The men are always seeking from women a little pillow to put their heads down on. They are always longing for the mother who held them as infants.” – Coco Chanel

22. “The fastest way to break the cycle of perfectionism and become a fearless mother is to give up the idea of doing it perfectly – indeed to embrace uncertainty and imperfection.” – Arianna Huffington

23. “Only mothers can think of the future because they give birth to it in their children.” – Maxim Grosky

24. “A mother’s arms are made of tenderness and children sleep soundly in them.” – Victor Hugo

25. “A mother is she who can take the place of all others but whose place no one else can take.” – Cardinal Mermillod

26. “Because I feel that, in the Heavens above, the angels, whispering to one another, can find, among their burning terms of love, none so devotional as that of ‘Mother.’” – Edgar Allen Poe (To My Mother)

27. “Birth is the epicenter of women’s power.” – Ani DiFranco

28. “Motherhood: All love begins and ends there.” – Robert Browning

29. “A mother’s happiness is like a beacon, lighting up the future but reflected also on the past in the guise of fond memories.” – Honore de Balzac

30. “The art of mothering is to teach the art of living to children.” – Elaine Heffner

31. “In the mother’s eyes, her smile, her stroking touch, the child reads the message: ‘You are there!’” – Adrienne Rich

32. “A mother is a person who, seeing there are only four pieces of pie for five people, promptly announces she never did care for pie.” – Tenneva Jordan

33. “A mother is the truest friend we have, when trials heavy and sudden fall upon us; when adversity takes the place of prosperity; when friends desert us; when trouble thickens around us, still will she cling to us, and endeavor by her kind precepts and counsels to dissipate the clouds of darkness, and cause peace to return to our hearts.” – Washington Irving

34. “Mothers hold their children’s hands for a short while, but their hearts forever.” – Unknown

35. “Mother’s love is peace. It need not be acquired, it need not be deserved.” – Erich Fromm

36. “Being a full-time mother is one of the highest salaried jobs since the payment is pure love.” – Mildred B. Vermont

37. “Giving birth and being born brings us into the essence of creation, where the human spirit is courageous and bold and the body, a miracle of wisdom.” – Harriette Hartigan

38. “Being a mom has made me so tired. And so happy.” – Tina Fey

39. “There is no velvet so soft as a mother’s lap, no rose as lovely as her smile, no path so flowery as that imprinted with her footsteps.” – Archibald Thompson

40. “A mother’s love is everything. It is what brings a child into this world. It is what molds their entire being. When a mother sees her child in danger, she is literally capable of anything. Mothers have lifted cars off of their children and destroyed entire dynasties. A mother’s love is the strongest energy known to man.” – Jamie McGuire
