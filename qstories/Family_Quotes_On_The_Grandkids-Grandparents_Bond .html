Family Quotes On The Grandkids-Grandparents Bond 

1. “Granddaughters grow a bond with their grandmothers. A bond that cannot be torn apart by anything. A grandmother will walk alongside them through it all. Help them through the highest points of their lives to the lowest. Grandmothers may pass, but the memories they left behind, will last forever.” – Unknown 

2. “A grandma and granddaughter always share a special bond, which is engraved on their hearts.” – Unknown 

3. “A mother becomes a true grandmother the day she stops noticing the terrible things her children do because she is so enchanted with the wonderful things her grandchildren do.” – Lois Wyse

4. “The reason grandchildren and grandparents get along so well is that they have a common enemy.” – Sam Levenson

5. “Perfect love sometimes does not come until grandchildren are born.” – Welsh Proverb

6. “Nobody can do for little children what grandparents do. Grandparents sort of sprinkle stardust over the lives of little children.” – Alex Haley

7. “There is no distance that can lessen the love of a grandma and her granddaughter.” – Unknown 

8. “A grandmother is God’s gift to her granddaughters.” – Unknown 

9. “A child needs a grandparent, anybody’s grandparent, to grow a little more securely into an unfamiliar world.” – Charles and Ann Morse

10. “Parent-child relationships are complex. Grandmother-grandchild relationships are simple. Grandmas are short on criticism and long on love.” – Janet Lanese

11. “Behind every granddaughter who believes in herself is a grandma who believed first.” – Unknown 

12. “Sometimes our grandmas and grandpas are like grand-angels.” – Lexie Saige

13. “Grandmother and granddaughter from the start. Best friends forever from the heart.” – Unknown 

14. “I live right in front of my daughter. I have a little house right in front of her because I can stay in touch. It’s like a little commune, and it’s very nice, because you can be close. I can see my granddaughter. I live very close to my brother, too, and my son. We’re a very close family.” – Debbie Reynolds

15. “Words are not enough to express the unconditional love that exists between a grandmother and her granddaughter.” – Unknown

16. “Grandchildren are the dots that connect the lines from generation to generation.” – Lois Wyse

17. “Grandchildren are precious gifts given to parents from their children.” – Unknown

18. “Grandchildren are loving reminders of what we’re really here for.” – Janet Lanese

19. “With your own children, you love them immediately― and with grandchildren, it’s exactly the same.” – Kevin Whately

20. “Side by side or miles apart, grandchildren are always close to the heart.” – Unknown
