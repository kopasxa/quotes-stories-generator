Best Birthday Wishes For Dad In Heaven

1. “Happy birthday in heaven to a caring and devoted father. I close my eyes and pretend I am giving you one last hug. I try to feel your strong arms around me and hear your voice telling me you love me. Your birthday will always be the day we are together in my mind.”

2. “Happy birthday to my Dad in heaven. You were our rock on earth and took on so much responsibility for others. But now you are in heaven where I hope you have found rest. Thank you for making me who I am, Dad. Rest in peace. Happy heavenly birthday.”

3. “You always did have the best birthday parties, and now you are having your birthday at the best venue in the universe. A party in heaven – there is no better place than that. Here’s to you, Dad, the man who was always the life of the party. I can’t wait to see you again.”

4. “I don’t know if you remember birthdays, but I just want you to know that you are special to me even though you are not here anymore. You are remembered in a special way, beyond ideas of time, or space, but in a place of the heart that has something in common with the reality of heaven. I love you. Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

5. “Thank you for everything you’ve done for us, always making us the priority in your life. You’ve taught us our self-worth so that we are somehow able to carry on without you. But although you taught us well, life here will never be as good as it was with you. Happy birthday, Daddy.”

6. “Dear dad, I sealed my birthday wishes for you inside an envelope filled with love and respect so it reaches you in Heaven and goes right through your soul.”

7. “Dad, you always said that life is short and that this is a school meant to teach us lessons before we go back home. I hope you are enjoying your birthday celebrations back home! Please save me a piece of cake because I want to be part of the celebration of you always.”

8. “I know you are still watching over me dad. I feel you almost every single day. This is especially true today because it is your birthday. I know I was not an easy kid to raise, but thanks to you I am proud of who I have become.”

9. “I am grateful to have had such a caring and loving Dad, and I find it remarkable that I can still feel your loving guidance even when you are in heaven. You have become a wise and caring voice inside my mind! Happy birthday, to a blessed man whom I will treasure in my heart and mind forever.”

10. “Enjoy every moment in heaven as you celebrate your birthday, Dad. I know that you are up there smiling, laughing, and cracking jokes. Every moment I spend with you is now an unforgettable memory. I will never forget you, ever.”

11. “Dad, I wish I could give you a hug and tell you how much you mean to me. I promise to live each day to the fullest and be the best person that I can be. Happy birthday in heaven.”

12. “Are you having the best celebration you’ve ever had, Dad? I bet it can’t get any better than the final destination – heaven! Nothing is the same without you, Dad, but I am trying to live as you taught me to. Happy birthday.”

13. “I couldn’t have had a better father. Thank you, Dad, for being there for me. I remember you today with especially fond memories. I love you very much. Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

14. “Thank you for being my dad and for preparing me so well for the journey of life on earth. I only hope that when the day comes you will be the one to prepare me for the next part of life, in heaven! I wish you a heavenly celebration today, Dad, with all my love. Happy birthday.”

15. “Dearest Dad in heaven, happy birthday. Now you know the absolute truth that we, here on earth, are still searching for. Have an extraordinary heavenly celebration of life everlasting, Dad!”

16. “Dad, you were the best father anyone could ever wish for. Even though you’re not here beside me, your lessons and guidance are with me every day. Happy birthday in heaven.”

17. “Death’s icy jaws may have snatched you away from me, but you will forever remain the hero of my life. Have a happy birthday in heaven, dad.”
