Baby’s Laughter Quotes

76. “When the first baby laughed for the first time, its laugh broke into a thousand pieces, and they all went skipping about, and that was the beginning of fairies.” – J. M. Barrie (Peter Pan)

77. “What can a baby’s laughter do? It can cleanse your soul and scatter a smile in your sad heart.” – Unknown

78. “The voice of an angel can be heard in a baby’s laugh.” – Unknown

79. “If one feels the need of something grand, something infinite, something that makes one feel aware of God, one need not go far to find it. I think that I see something deeper, more infinite, more eternal than the ocean in the expression of the eyes of a little baby when it wakes in the morning and coos or laughs because it sees the sun shining on its cradle.” – Vincent van Gogh

80. “The best feeling in the world is watching your own baby break into laughter.” – Unknown

81. “When the babies laugh, it comes all the way from way down in their toes. So I figure there must be something pretty special hidden down there.” – Debora Geary

82. “I think the most beautiful sound is a child laughing.” – David Thewlis

83. “The most precious sight, the smile of a child. The most precious sound, a child’s laughter.” – Unknown

84. “Bring a baby to any grown-up – even the gruffest or most inhibited – and watch what happens. Without exception, they will do something to try and make the baby laugh. Psychologists, anthropologists, and other experts have theories about why this is so. For me it’s enough to believe that whenever a baby laughs, our humanity is somehow exalted.” – Jack Moore

85. “My fingers are tickled to delight by the soft ripple of a baby’s laugh…” – Helen Keller

86. “The tots both started laughing. On the same day. I’m now obsessed with getting them to do it. Babies laughing is like opium.” – Neil Patrick Harris

87. “Ever wondered how an angel’s voice sounds? Try listening to the sound of a baby’s laughter.” – Unknown

88. “Babies are for bouncing and for jouncing on your knee. For charming you, disarming you, and giggling with glee.” – Unknown

89. “With giggles from a waterfall and breezes passing by, God made a baby’s laughter and a tiny, sleepy sigh.” – Unknown
