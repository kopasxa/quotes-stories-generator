Inspirational Quotes About Giving Birth

1. “We have a secret in our culture, and it’s not that birth is painful. It’s that women are strong.” – Laura Stavoe Harm

2. “Birthing is the most profound initiation to spirituality a woman can have.” – Robin Lim

3. “Labor is also teamwork. It is a mother and baby learning together how to push and how to be born, how to yield and separate from the union of pregnancy. You are not in control nor are you out of control during labor. The best way to approach labor is with an attitude of learning rather than controlling.” – Gayle Peterson

4. “Birth is a rite of passage of women. Their journey should be honored, their rights should be fiercely protected, and their stories should be shared.” – Marcie Macari

5. “There are no words that can describe the euphoria you feel when your baby recognizes you for the first time and smiles.” – Jared Padalecki

6. “Birth is the sudden opening of a window, through which you look out upon a stupendous prospect. For what has happened? A miracle. You have exchanged nothing for the possibility of everything.” – William MacNeile Dixon

7. “There is such a special sweetness in being able to participate in creation.” – Pamela S. Nadav

8. “The wisdom and compassion a woman can intuitively experience in childbirth can make her a source of healing and understanding for other women.” – Stephen Gaskin

9. “There is power that comes to women when they give birth. They don’t ask for it, it simply invades them. Accumulates like clouds on the horizon and passes through, carrying the child with it.” – Sheryl Feldman

10. “The major role that the body’s natural oxytocin plays in birth encourages the idea that birth is an experience of love… Birth is a spiritual experience simply because it is largely an act of love on the part of the body physiology and the indwelling spirit.” – Cathy Daub

11. “Childbirth is more admirable than conquest, more amazing than self-defense, and as courageous as either one.” – Gloria Steinem

12. “Giving birth to your child doesn’t only give you a cute little baby to love for the rest of your life but it also strengthens your bond with your husband as a couple.” – Unknown

13. “Giving birth in ecstasy: This is our birthright and our body’s intent. Mother Nature, in her wisdom, prescribes birthing hormones that take us outside (ec) our usual state (stasis), so that we can be transformed on every level as we enter motherhood.” – Sarah Buckley

14. “Birth isn’t something we suffer, but something we actively do and exult in.” – Sheila Kitzinger

15. “Birth is an experience that demonstrates that life is not merely function and utility, but form and beauty.” – Christopher Largen

16. “The moment a child is born, the mother is also born. She never existed before. The woman existed, but the mother, never. A mother is something absolutely new.” – Rajneesh

17. “Attending births is like growing roses. You have to marvel at the ones that just open up and bloom at the first kiss of the sun but you wouldn’t dream of pulling open the petals of the tightly closed buds and forcing them to blossom to your time line.” – Gloria Lemay

18. “Birth is an opportunity to transcend. To rise above what we are accustomed to, reach deeper inside ourselves than we are familiar with, and to see not only what we are truly made of, but the strength we can access in and through birth.” – Marcie Macari

19. “If we want to find safe alternatives to obstetrics, we must rediscover midwifery. To rediscover midwifery is the same as giving back childbirth to women. And imagine the future if surgical teams were at the service of the midwives and the women instead of controlling them.” – Michel Odent

20. “The birth of a child is a joy to the parent and the world.” – Lailah Gifty Akita

21. “Birth is a mystery. Words are not enough.” – Marie O’Connor

22. “When enough women realize that birth is a time of great opportunity to get in touch with their true power, and when they are willing to assume responsibility for this, we will reclaim the power of birth and help move technology where it belongs – in the service of birthing women, not their master.” – Christiane Northrup

23. “A woman in birth is at once her most powerful, and most vulnerable. But any woman who has birthed unhindered understands that we are stronger than we know.” – Marcie Macari

24. “So the question remains. Is childbirth painful? Yes. It can be, along with a thousand amazing sensations for which we have yet to find adequate language. Every birth is different, and every woman’s experience and telling of her story will be unique.” – Marcie Macari

25. “The day of birth is a sacred day.” – Lailah Gifty Akita

26. “All natural birth has a purpose and a plan; who would think of tearing open the chrysalis as the butterfly is emerging? Who would break the shell to pull the chick out?” – Marie Mongon

27. “To celebrate the birth of new life, is to appreciate the miraculous work of the Creator.” – Wayne Chirisa

28. “Giving birth to your first child is the closest you can ever come to doing magic.” – Unknown

29. “Having your first child feels like smelling the lilies from a spiritual garden.” – Unknown

30. “A woman who has just given birth to her first child is like an angel sent to Earth by God to shower a newborn baby with pure love and affection.” – Unknown

31. “Carrying her first child is the biggest reward that God can give to a woman.” – Unknown

32. “You will never know the amount of love you can give to somebody until you give birth to your first child.” – Unknown

33. “Your firstborn baby’s arms are the most precious jewel you can ever have hanging around your neck.” – Unknown

34. “You start understanding life once a baby starts to grow inside your belly.” – Unknown

35. “If you don’t believe in love at first sight, wait until you give birth to your first baby.” – Unknown

36. “Every newborn baby is its mother’s definition of perfect.” – Unknown

37. “I am pretty sure that the day I gave birth to my first child, angels clapped and the moon danced with the stars.” – Unknown

38. “If I have to pick the best day of my life, it will be the day I gave birth to my first child without any second thoughts.” – Unknown

39. “The first breath of every child takes away the breath of its parents.” – Unknown
