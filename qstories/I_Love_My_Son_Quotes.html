I Love My Son Quotes

1. “The birth of my sons. The birth of new emotions… what a privilege. What a blessing.” – Elle Macpherson

2. “There is an enduring tenderness in the love of a mother to a son that transcends all other affections of the heart.” – Washington Irving

3. “To a mother, a son is never a fully grown man; and a son is never a fully grown man until he understands and accepts this about his mother.” – Unknown

4. “I have a son, who is my heart. A wonderful young man, daring and loving and strong and kind.” – Maya Angelou

5. “The only man who has stolen my heart is my son.” – Sandra Bullock

6. “Though our new son is barely a part of our present, we look at his squirming, warm bundle of future potential and say to ourselves, ‘Hello, little man.’ Little do we know the adventure we are beginning!” – Dr. Gregory L. Jantz

7. “And she loved a little boy very much, even more than she loved herself.” – Shel Silverstein

8. “Let your boys test their wings. They may not be eagles, but that doesn’t mean they shouldn’t soar free.” – C. J. Milbrandt

9. “Sons are the anchors of a mother’s life.” – Sophocles

10. “The most important mark I will leave on this world is my son.” – Sarah Shaki

11. “Until you have a son of your own, you will never know what that means. You will never know the joy beyond joy, the love beyond feeling that resonates in the heart of a father as he looks upon his son.” – Kent Nerburn

12. “Yet now in my arms, I was holding a helpless baby boy who would grow into a man… I cannot imagine that soft little face one day having whiskers.” – Rhonda Stoppe

13. “My son’s the most precious thing to me; he’s changed me from being selfish to selfless.” – Ricardo Antonio Chavira

14. “I live for my sons. I would be lost without them.” – Princess Diana

15. “A mother’s love doesn’t make her son more dependent and timid; it actually makes him stronger and more independent.” – Cheri Fuller
