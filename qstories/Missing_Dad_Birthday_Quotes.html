1. “Thank you for giving me enough love, wisdom, warmth, and memories to last a lifetime. I miss you every day, but I know that you’re still with me. Happy birthday in heaven Dad!”

4. “I miss you everyday, dad, but today I miss you even more. You’ll always be in my heart. Happy birthday.”

5. “Happy birthday to my incredible Dad. Although you are no longer with us, your memory lives on. We all miss you, but know that eventually we will all be reunited in heaven. We will still be reminiscing on the amazing memories you left us with for years to come.”

6. “Many birthday greetings from all those you left behind on earth. We love and miss you dearly, daddy. Happy heavenly birthday.”

7. “Dad, on days like your birthday, I miss you even more than usual. Instead of being sad, I try to focus on the priceless time we had together, and the incredible memories we made. You gave so much of yourself to me, and that will never go away. I hope you can hear me and feel my love. I look forward to being with you again one day.”

8. “Dad, I miss you every day and I’m so grateful for my memories of you. Happy birthday in heaven.”

9. “Happy Birthday, Dad. Not a single day goes by without me thinking about you. I miss you more than words could ever hope to express. I am especially thinking of you today, on your birthday. I know that you are smiling down at me and enjoying yourself. I will always love you.”

11. “Today I remember all the wonderful birthdays we had together. I miss you beyond words. I hope you’re doing well and I will see you again someday. Happy birthday in Heaven Dad.”

12. “Happy birthday in heaven to my wonderful dad. I love you and miss you so much. Every day I’m a better person because you raised me to appreciate all that is good in myself and those around me.”

13. “Someday, when I see you again, I’ll give you the biggest hug and tell you how much I’ve missed you. Happy birthday in heaven Daddy.”

14. “Happy Birthday, my Dad in heaven. We remember and miss you, Dad. On every birthday of yours, we still celebrate the legendary life you’ve lived and the remarkable gifts you’ve left for us in our hearts. You live forever in our hearts.”

15. “How blessed I was to be given a father who loved and cared for me from day one. Happy birthday in heaven. I miss you and love you so much.”

16. “Dad, even though I miss you each day, I’m so much stronger because of everything you taught me, and so much more confident because of all the love you gave me. Happy birthday in heaven.”

17. “Happy Birthday to my father who is now in heaven. You became one with the All and I don’t know if you still celebrate birthdays. But I am still human, Dad, and I will continue to miss you on every birthday, every Sunday, and every single moment of my human life. Happy birthday.”

18. “My amazing Dad, happy heavenly birthday. I’m grateful to know you are in heaven, but I find earth a little hard to handle without you! Ask those beautiful angels to send me the strength to go on. You’ve always known how to make things better! I miss you, and I still need you. Have a blessed heavenly birthday, Dad.”

19. “My loving Dad in heaven, happy birthday. There is no better place to be than in heaven with the almighty Father. May your party be felt across the universe, and may you continue to spread love as you did here on earth. I love and miss you so much!”
