Funny Family Quotes

1. “The only things kids wear out faster than their shoes are their parents.” – John J. Plomp

2. “Before I had kids, I always found it funny how people would talk about their children like they were the cutest things on the planet and how every little thing they did was endlessly fascinating. Now that I’ve had kids, I can say with certainty that, my children really are the cutest things on this planet and every little thing they do is endlessly fascinating.” – Jennifer Miller

3. “A two-year-old is kind of like having a blender, but you don’t have a top for it.” – Jerry Seinfeld

4. “24/7. Once you sign on to be a mother, that’s the only shift they offer!” – Jodi Picoult

5. “Sometimes going to bed feels like the highlight of my day. Ironically, to my children, bedtime is a punishment that violates their basic rights as human beings.” – Jim Gaffigan

6. “Cleaning your house while your kids are still growing up is like shoveling the sidewalk before it stops snowing.” – Phyllis Diller

7. “The quickest way for a parent to get a child’s attention is to sit down and look comfortable.” – Lane Olinghouse

8. “When you have a baby, sleep is not an option. You can’t sleep. Even on vacation, you wake up at 6:30 a.m.” – Jimmy Fallon

9. “My mom used to say it doesn’t matter how many kids you have… because one kid will take up 100% of your time so more kids can’t possibly take up more than 100% of your time.” – Karen Brown

10. “Becoming a mom to me means that you have accepted that for the next 16 years of your life, you will have a sticky purse.” – Nia Vardalos

11. “It just occurred to me that the majority of my diet is made up of the foods that my kid didn’t finish.” – Carrie Underwood

12. “You go through big chunks of time where you’re just thinking, ‘This is impossible, this is impossible.’ And then you just keep going and keep going, and you sort of do the impossible.” – Tina Fey

13. “There are three ways to get something done: do it yourself, hire someone to do it, or ask your kids not to do it.” – Malcolm Kushner

14. “Before I got married I had six theories about raising children. Now, I have six children and no theories.” – John Wilmot

15. “Having children is like living in a frat house – nobody sleeps, everything’s broken, and there’s a lot of throwing up.” – Ray Romano

16. “Waking your kids up for school the first day after a break is almost as much fun as birthing them was.” – Jenny McCarthy

17. “Having one child makes you a parent. Having two kids makes you a referee.” – David Frost

18. “Mother Nature is providential. She gives us twelve years to develop a love for our children before turning them into teenagers.” – William Galvin

19. “If your baby is beautiful and perfect, never cries or fusses, sleeps on schedule and burps on demand, an angel all the time, you’re the grandma.” – Theresa Bloomingdale

20. “A baby changes your dinner party conversation from politics to poops.” – Maurice Johnston

21. “You can learn many things from children. How much patience you have, for instance.” – Franklin P. Adams
