Older Siblings Quotes

1. “I can’t promise to solve all your problems, but I can promise you won’t have to face them alone.” – Unknown

2. “As your brother, I always know that you, my sister, look out for me. And as your younger brother, I also know you will always be older than me.” – Theodore W. Higginsworth

3. “Older siblings… the only people who will pick on you for their own entertainment and beat up anyone else who tries.” – Unknown

4. “Be grateful when your older brothers growl at you, and when they tell you what to do and what not to do. Beautiful, they are just trying to fulfill their duty of protecting you.” – Unknown

5. “That’s the function of big brothers… to help their little sisters when their worlds are collapsing.” – Susan Beth Pfeffer

6. “I’ve been the oldest child since before you were born.” – E.L. Konigsburg
