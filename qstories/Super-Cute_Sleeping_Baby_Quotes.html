Super-Cute Sleeping Baby Quotes

1. “I wonder what my baby dreams about? It must be magical.” – Unknown

2. “With a butterfly kiss and a ladybug hug, sleep tight, little one, like a bug in a rug.” – Unknown

3. “Babies smile in their sleep because they’re listening to the whispering of angels.” – Unknown

4. “I could watch you for a single minute and find a thousand things that I love about you.” – Unknown

18. “Sleep tight, my little one!” – Unknown

19. “Happiness is when you’re singing lullabies as you watch your baby fall asleep.” – Debasish Mridha

20. “Despite having a million things to do, it is very difficult to look away from my sleeping baby. She is like a magnet!” – Maria Jose Ovalle

21. “When she is sleeping and yawns my heart melts.” – Unknown

22. “A mother’s arms are made of tenderness and children sleep soundly in them.” – Victor Hugo
