59. “Grandpa: The guy that always gives you the extra scoop of ice cream.” – Unknown

63. “You know you’re a grandma when… You used to get angry at your kids and now you laugh when your grandkids do the same thing.” – Unknown

64. “Grandpa’s basement – you never know what you’ll find there.” – Unknown
