Inspirational Mother’s Love Quotes

1. “Mother’s love is peace. It need not be acquired, it need not be deserved.” – Erich Fromm

2. “A mother’s love doesn’t make her son more dependent and timid; it actually makes him stronger and more independent.” – Cheri Fuller

3. “Mother’s love is the fuel that enables a normal human being to do the impossible.” – Unknown

4. “The natural state of motherhood is unselfishness.” – Jessica Lange

5. “How beautifully everything is arranged by Nature; as soon as a child enters the world, it finds a mother ready to take care of it.” – Jules Michelet

6. “A mother is the only woman who is able to understand your tears without judging you.” – Unknown

7. “The love of a mother is the veil of a softer light between the heart and the heavenly Father.” – Samuel Taylor Coleridge

8. “The heart of a mother is a deep abyss at the bottom of which you will always find forgiveness.” – Honore de Balzac

9. “I remember my mother’s prayers, and they have always followed me. They have clung to me all my life.” – Abraham Lincoln

10. “A mother is the truest friend we have, when trials, heavy and sudden, fall upon us; when adversity takes the place of prosperity; when friends who rejoice with us in our sunshine, desert us when troubles thicken around us, still will she cling to us, and endeavor by her kind precepts and counsels to dissipate the clouds of darkness, and cause peace to return to our hearts.” – Washington Irving

11. “A society without mothers would not only be a cold society, but a society that has lost its heart, lost the feel of home.” – Pope Francis

12. “A mother’s arms are more comforting than anyone else’s.” – Princess Diana

13. “A mother’s love for her child is like nothing else in the world. It knows no law, no pity. It dares all things and crushes down remorselessly all that stands in its path.” – Agatha Christie

14. “A mother is she who can take the place of all others but whose place no one else can take.” – Cardinal Mermillod

15. “You may take offense at your mother. But always remember: your mother is the only woman who will love you in spite of all your disadvantages.” – Unknown

16. “A mother understands what a child does not say.” – Jewish Proverb

17. “There is no greater privilege in living than bringing a tiny new human being into the world and then trying to raise him or her properly during the next eighteen years.” – James C. Dobson

18. “Moms are as relentless as the tides. They don’t just drive us to practice, they drive us to greatness.” – Steve Rushin

19. “My daughter and two sons are the flowers in my heart and garden.” – Ana Monnar

20. “Mothers can forgive anything! Tell me all, and be sure that I will never let you go, though the whole world should turn from you.” – Louisa May Alcott

21. “There’s no way to be a perfect mother and a million ways to be a good one.” – Jill Churchill

22. “Moms are people who know us the best and love us the most.” – Unknown

23. “A mother’s arms are made of tenderness and children sleep soundly in them.” – Victor Hugo

24. “Maybe your mother isn’t able to give you everything you want. But she is able to give you even more – her love.” – Unknown

25. “Some mothers are kissing mothers and some are scolding mothers, but it is love just the same, and most mothers kiss and scold together.” – Pearl S. Buck

26. “The influence of a mother in the lives of her children is beyond calculation.” – James E. Faust
