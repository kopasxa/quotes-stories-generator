Daddy’s Little Girl Quotes

58. “Fathers and daughters have a special bond. She is always daddy’s little girl.” – Richard L. Ratliff

59. “I think my mom put it best. She said, ‘Little girls soften their daddy’s hearts.'” Paul Walker

60. “My little girls are the most beautiful women in the world. I am a lucky, lucky man. I will spend every day making sure that they know this.” – Russell Simmons

61. “To her, the name of father was another name for love.” – Fanny Fern (Fresh Leaves)

62. “They say that from the instant he lays eyes on her, a father adores his daughter. Whoever she grows up to be, she is always to him that little girl in pigtails. She makes him feel like Christmas. In exchange, he makes a secret promise not to see the awkwardness of her teenage years, the mistakes she makes or the secrets she keeps.” – Unknown

63. “There’s two things I know for sure: She was sent here from heaven and she’s daddy’s little girl.” – Bob Carlisle (Butterfly Kisses)

64. “A father’s job is not to teach his daughter how to be a lady. It’s to teach her how a lady should be treated.” – Unknown

65. “And there will always be a part of me nobody else is ever gonna see but you and me. My little girl.” – Ben Folds (Gracie)

66. “A man’s daughter is his heart. Just with feet, walking out in the world.” – Mat Johnson

67. “Sometimes you’re asleep I whisper ‘I Love You!’ in the moonlight at your door. As I walk away, I hear you say, ‘Daddy, Love You More!'” – Tim McGraw (My Little Girl)

68. “When I cried, he cried with me. When I was doubtful, he believed in me. When I did well, he cheered me on. When I messed up, he told me I was wrong. Every now and then when I went against his will, he punished me, but he praised me still. He taught me how to forgive, even when I was right. He never liked war, he was never one to fight. I’ll always be a daddy’s girl, I have the best daddy in the whole wide world.” – Charmaine J. Forde

69. “There’s no better feeling than making your little girls laugh.” – Jerry O’Connell

70. “When I come home, my daughter will run to the door and give me a big hug, and everything that’s happened that day just melts away.” – Hugh Jackman

71. “Dad: a daughter’s first love.” – Unknown

72. “She is just a baby, she’s my little girl. She looks like her mommy, sporting little curls. She’s got lots to learn, though she is sure to know, that I’ll quietly spot her, anywhere she goes.” – Clark Richard (Red Robin)

73. “You fathers will understand. You have a little girl. She looks up to you. You’re her oracle. You’re her hero. And then the day comes when she gets her first permanent wave and goes to her first real party, and from that day on, you’re in a constant state of panic.” – Stanley T. Banks

74. “Oh, with all that I’ve done wrong, I must have done something right, to deserve a hug every morning and butterfly kisses at night.” – Bob Carlisle (Butterfly Kisses)

75. “A little girl giggles when she is denied an ice-cream by her mother. She knows daddy will get her some later.” – Unknown

76. “A father holds his daughter’s hand for a short while, but he holds her heart forever.” – Unknown

77. “There’s something like a line of gold thread running through a man’s words when he talks to his daughter, and gradually over the years it gets to be long enough for you to pick up in your hands and weave into a cloth that feels like love itself.” – John Gregory Brown
