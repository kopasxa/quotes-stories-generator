Beautiful Quotes For Mom

1. “Motherhood is a choice you make every day to put someone else’s happiness and well-being ahead of your own, to teach the hard lessons, to do the right thing even when you’re not sure what the right thing is… and to forgive yourself over and over again for doing everything wrong.” – Donna Bell

2. “You are breathtakingly beautiful with each year you age, and I promise I’m not just saying this because it’s your birthday.” – Unknown

3. “A mother is the truest friend we have when trials heavy and sudden fall upon us; when friends who rejoice with us in our sunshine desert us; when trouble thickens around us, still she will cling to us, and endeavor by her kind precepts and counsels to dissipate the clouds of darkness, and cause peace to return to our hearts.” – Washington Irving

4. “My mother, my friend so dear, throughout my life you’re always near. A tender smile to guide my way, you’re the sunshine to light my day.” – Unknown

5. “There is no limit to how much I love you, Mom. Just like how much you have loved and cared for every single one of us.” – Unknown

6. “As mothers and daughters, we are connected with one another. My mother is the bones of my spine, keeping me straight and true. She is my blood, making sure it runs rich and strong. She is the beating of my heart. I cannot now imagine a life without her. ” – Kristin Hannah

7. “As I travel these seas called life, you are the constant lighthouse that leads me through the storms to the shore.” – Unknown

8. “I am closest to my mother, as she is my rock, my pillar of strength, and my world. Not only has she stood by me through all times – happy, sad, and otherwise – but there have even been moments when I had completely lost hope, and her immense belief in me had lifted me up.” – Amruta Khanvilkar

9. “No language can express the power and beauty and heroism of a mother’s love.” – Edwin Chapin

10. “God could not be everywhere and therefore He made mothers.” – Jewish Proverb

11. “Successful mothers are not the ones who have never struggled. They are the ones who never give up, despite their struggles.” – Sharon Jaynes

12. “Mother’s love is bliss, is peace, it need not be acquired, it need not be deserved. If it is there, it is like a blessing; if it is not there, it is as if all the beauty had gone out of life.” – Erich Fromm
