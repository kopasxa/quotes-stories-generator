Best Quotes On Grandparents

1. “A grandmother is a safe haven.” – Suzette Haden Elgin

2. “To keep the heart unwrinkled, to be hopeful, kindly, cheerful, reverent that is to triumph over old age.” – Thomas Bailey Aldrich

3. “Truth be told, being a grandma is as close as we ever get to perfection. The ultimate warm sticky bun with plump raisins and nuts. Clouds nine, ten, and eleven.” – Bryna Nelson Paston

4. “Sometimes our grandmas and grandpas are like grand-angels.” – Lexie Saige

5. “Grandparents make the world a little softer, a little kinder, and a little warmer.” – Unknown

6. “Beautiful young people are accidents of nature, but beautiful old people are works of art.” – Marjory Barslow-Greenbie

7. “I wish I had the energy that my grandchildren have – if only for self-defense.” – Gene Perret

8. “Nobody can do for little children what grandparents do. Grandparents sort of sprinkle stardust over the lives of little children.” – Alex Haley

9. “Varicose veins are the result of an improper selection of grandparents.” – William Osler

10. “A child needs a grandparent, anybody’s grandparent, to grow a little more securely into an unfamiliar world.” – Charles and Ann Morse

11. “Grandchildren are the dots that connect the lines from generation to generation.” – Lois Wyse

12. “Uncles and aunts, and cousins, are all very well, and fathers and mothers are not to be despised; but a grandmother, at holiday time, is worth them all.” – Fanny Fern

13. “Surely, two of the most satisfying experiences in life must be those of being a grandchild or a grandparent.” – Donald A. Norberg

14. “I loved their home. Everything smelled older, worn but safe; the food aroma had baked itself into the furniture.” – Susan Strasberg

15. “It is as grandmothers that our mothers come into the fullness of their grace.” – Christopher Morley

16. “Posterity is the patriotic name for grandchildren.” – Art Linkletter

17. “If you’re lucky enough to still have grandparents, visit them, cherish them and celebrate them while you can.” – Regina Brett

18. “The best place to be when you’re sad is Grandpa’s lap.” – Unknown 

19. “Her grandmother, as she gets older, is not fading but rather becoming more concentrated.” – Paulette Bates Alden

20. “If your baby is ‘beautiful and perfect, never cries or fusses, sleeps on schedule and burps on demand, an angel all the time,’ you’re the grandma.” – Teresa Bloomingdale

21. “A grandmother pretends she doesn’t know who you are on Halloween.” – Erma Bombeck

22. “A grandparent is a little bit parent, a little bit teacher, and a little bit best friend.” – Unknown

23. “Everyone needs to have access both to grandparents and grandchildren in order to be a full human being.” – Margaret Mead

24. “You are the sun, Grandma, you are the sun in my life.” – Kitty Tsui

25. “I feel like my grandparents and parents gave me a tremendous amount. And if I can pass some of that on, then I’ll be very happy.” – Caroline Kennedy

26. “The history of our grandparents is remembered not with rose petals but in the laughter and tears of their children and their children’s children. It is into us that the lives of grandparents have gone. It is in us that their history becomes a future.” – Charles & Ann Morse

27. “As you are now, so once were we.” – James Joyce

28. “Because (grandparents) are usually free to love and guide and befriend the young without having to take daily responsibility for them. They can often reach out past pride and fear of failure and close the space between generations.” – Jimmy Carter

29. “We should all have one person who knows how to bless us despite the evidence. Grandmother was that person to me.” – Phyllis Theroux

30. “Every generation revolts against its fathers and makes friends with its grandfathers.” – Lewis Mumford

31. “One of the most powerful handclaps is that of a new grandbaby around the finger of a grandfather.” – Joy Hargrove

32. “Have children while your parents are still young enough to take care of them.” – Rita Rudner

33. “An hour with your grandchildren can make you feel young again. Anything longer than that, and you start to age quickly.” – Gene Perret

34. “I know you’ve loved me since I was born, but I’ve loved you my whole life.” – Unknown

35. “Grandparents, like heroes, are as necessary to a child’s growth as vitamins.” – Joyce Allston

36. “If nothing is going well, call your grandmother.” – Italian proverb

37. “Grandparents always have their door open when you are in need of a hug.” – Unknown

38. “Appreciate your grandparents while they are around. Listen to their stories, cherish their love and spend time with them. It is as good for you as it is for them.” – Unknown
