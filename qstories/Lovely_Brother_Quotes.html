Lovely Brother Quotes

1. “Never make a companion equal to a brother.” – Hesiod

2. “Being pretty on the inside means you don’t hit your brother and you eat all your peas. That’s what my grandma taught me.” – Lord Chesterfield

3. “I sought my soul, but my soul I could not see. I sought my God, but my God eluded me. I sought my brother, and I found all three.” – Baba Amte

5. “Of all the things I have to play, I’d choose my brother any day.” – Unknown

6. “The highlight of my childhood was making my brother laugh so hard that food came out of his nose.” – Garrison Keillor

7. “What strange creatures brothers are!” – Jane Austen

10. “The best thing about having four big brothers is you always have someone to do something for you.” – Chloe Moretz

11. “There is no other love like the love for a brother. There is no other love like the love from a brother.” – Terri Guillemets

12. “When brothers agree, no fortress is so strong as their common life.” – Antisthenes

13. “We are not only our brother’s keeper; in countless large and small ways, we are our brother’s maker.” – Bonaro Overstreet

14. “A brother shares childhood memories and grown-up dreams.” – Unknown

15. “We must learn together as brothers or perish together as fools.” – Martin Luther King Jr.

16. “A brother is a friend given by nature.” – Jean Baptiste Legouve

17. “Help your brother’s boat across and your own will reach the shore.” – Hindu Proverb

18. “There’s no buddy like a brother!” – Unknown

19. “There is a little boy inside the man who is my brother. Oh, how I hated that little boy. And how I love him too.” – Anna Quindla

20. “Sometimes being a brother is even better than being a superhero.” – Marc Brown

21. “Brothers are just born to bother sisters.” – Unknown

22. “We came into the world like brother and brother; And now let’s go hand in hand, not before one another.” – William Shakespeare

23. “Half the time when brothers wrestle, it’s just an excuse to hug each other.” – James Patterson
