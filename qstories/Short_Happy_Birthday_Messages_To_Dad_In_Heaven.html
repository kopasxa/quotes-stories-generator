Short Happy Birthday Messages To Dad In Heaven

1. “Happy birthday in heaven to the funniest, silliest, most hilarious dad I was ever fortunate enough to have.”

2. “Happy birthday in heaven to a truly fantastic dad. Thank you for everything you gave me, and for making me who I am today.”

3. “Dad, there will always be warmth in my heart and a smile on my face when I think of our time together. Happy birthday in heaven.”

4. ” When I think of you, I always smile because I know that’s what you would want. Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

5. “Happy birthday in heaven to the most caring, compassionate, loving, wonderful father anyone could ever ask for.”

6. “Enjoy the peace and love of heavenly birthdays. I will always love you. Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

7. “Every day I carry the lessons that you gave me in my heart, and I strive to be the person who would make you proud. Happy birthday Dad, love you.”

8. “Happy birthday wishes seem to disappear with the wind, but I know your soul hears them.”

9. “Someday, when I see you again, I’ll give you the biggest hug and tell you how much I’ve missed you. Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

10. “I rejoice in the memories of such a wonderful father and celebrate the man you were. Happy birthday in heaven. I love you very much.”

11. “Happy birthday in heaven, Dad. You made me who I am today, and I hope I’ve made you proud.”

12. “Happy birthday in heaven, Dad. May heaven always bless and keep you. You are always in my heart and I will forever be proud to be your child.”

13. “Wishing the warmest happy birthday in heaven to the kindest and dearest dad. Thank you for all of the wonderful memories we made together as a family, you are my brightest star!”

14. “Even though it hurts that he’s no longer here with me, I am so blessed for all of the time we spent together. Happy birthday in heaven, Dad.”

15. “Happy birthday in heaven, Dad. I’m so blessed to have been given such wonderful memories of an amazing man.”
