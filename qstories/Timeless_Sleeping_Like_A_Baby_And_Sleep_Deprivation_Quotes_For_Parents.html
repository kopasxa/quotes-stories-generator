Timeless ‘Sleeping Like A Baby’ And Sleep Deprivation Quotes For Parents

1. “When you have a baby, sleep is not an option. You can’t sleep. Even on vacation, you wake up at 6:30 am.” – Jimmy Fallon

2. “I don’t want to “sleep like a baby,” I want to sleep like my husband.” – Unknown

3. “People who say they sleep like a baby usually don’t have one.” – Leo. J. Burke

4. “I’m sleeping like a baby, too. Every two hours, I wake up, screaming.” – Colin Powell

5. “Some nights I sleep like a baby. Other nights it’s, Oh God, I just came up with a bomb shot.” – Michael Bay

6. “The sleep deprivation after children is so real. I liken it to what it must feel like to walk on the moon and cry the whole time because you had heard that the moon was supposed to be great but in truth it totally sucks.” – Amy Poehler

7. “Sleep? Yes, I have a vague recollection of what that was like.” – Unknown

8. “Like every other mother on the planet, from the moment my first baby entered the house, I stopped getting real sleep. Motherhood means I’m always a little bit awake, a little bit alert at all times. One eye open.” – Shonda Rhimes

9. “I’m a baby. I sleep like a baby – I’m up every two hours. And I think a lot. I worry a lot. I have great nights of no sleep where ideas come.” – Billy Crystal

10. “Like when people say they slept like a baby. Do they mean they slept well? Or do they mean they woke up every ten minutes, screaming?” – Lee Child

11. “It was that sort of sleep in which you wake every hour and think to yourself that you have not been sleeping at all; you can remember dreams that are like reflections, daytime thinking slightly warped.” – Kim Stanley Robinson

12. “Not being able to sleep is terrible. You have the misery of having partied all night… without the satisfaction.” – Lynn Johnston

13. “Mothers are basically part of a scientific experiment to prove that sleep is not a crucial part of human life.” – Unknown

14. “Being a mother means your baby is the one up all night drinking and you get to be hungover the next day.” – Unknown

15. “First thing in the morning, we’re really tired, and we look at each other and we wonder, ‘Are we ever going to get sleep?’ And yet, it doesn’t matter if you don’t get sleep. It’s an honor to take care of them.” – Angelina Jolie

16. “You know you’re a mom when your fantasies are about sleeping.” – Unknown

17. “Parenting is a constant battle between going to bed to catch up on some sleep or staying awake to finally get some alone time.” – Unknown

18. “Friend: Are you getting enough sleep? Me: Sometimes when I sneeze, my eyes close.” –Unknown

19. “I finally got 8 hours of sleep. Took me four days but whatever.” – Unknown

20. “My morning routine includes ten minutes of me sitting on my bed and thinking of how tired I am.” – Unknown
