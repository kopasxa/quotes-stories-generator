Short Kindness Quotes

1. “You can accomplish by kindness what you cannot by force.” – Publilius Syrus

2. “When words are both true and kind, they can change the world.” – Buddha

3. “Be kind, for everyone you meet is fighting a hard battle.” – Plato

4. “Wherever there is a human being, there is an opportunity for a kindness.” – Lucius Annaeus Seneca

5. “I feel the capacity to care is the thing which gives life its deepest significance.” – Pablo Casals

6. “You cannot do kindness too soon, for you never know how soon it will be too late.” – Ralph Waldo Emerson

7. “Being considerate of others will take your children further in life than any college degree.” – Marian Wright Edelman

8. “Treat everyone with politeness and kindness, not because they’re nice, but because you are.” – Roy T. Bennett

9. “The words of kindness are more healing to a drooping heart than balm or honey.” – Sarah Fielding

10. “Kindness can become its own motive. We are made kind by being kind.” – Eric Hoffer

11. “One who knows how to show and to accept kindness will be a friend better than any possession.” – Sophocles

12. “Kindness is loving yourself enough to love those around you.” – Raktivist

13. “Unexpected kindness is the most powerful, least costly, and most underrated agent of human change.” – Bob Kerrey

14. “I’ve been searching for ways to heal myself, and I’ve found that kindness is the best way.” – Lady Gaga

15. “Kindness is the light that dissolves all walls between souls, families, and nations.” – Paramahansa Yogananda

16. “Every sunrise is an invitation for us to arise and brighten someone’s day.” – Richelle E. Goodrich

17. “I feel that there is nothing more truly artistic than to love people.” – Vincent Van Gogh

18. “An eye for an eye, and the whole world would be blind.” – Kahlil Gibran

19. “When I was young, I admired clever people. Now that I am old, I admire kind people.” – Abraham Joshua Heschel

20. “A negative mind will never give you a positive life.” – Ziad K. Abdelnour

21. “How beautiful a day can be when kindness touches it!” – George Elliston

22. “Extend yourself in kindness to other human beings wherever you can.” – Oprah Winfrey

23. “A great man shows his greatness by the way he treats little men.” – Thomas Carlyle

24. “In a gentle way, you can shake the world.” – Mahatma Gandhi

25. “Be kind, for everyone you meet is fighting a battle you know nothing about.” – Wendy Mass

26. “Kindness gives birth to kindness.” – Sophocles

27. “True beauty is born through our actions and aspirations and in the kindness we offer to others.” – Alek Wek

28. “If you want to lift yourself up, lift someone else up.” – Booker T. Washington

29. “Never look down on anybody unless you’re helping them up.” – Jesse Jackson

30. “What wisdom can you find that is greater than kindness?” – Jean-Jacques Rousseau

31. “Kindness is in our power, even when fondness is not.” – Samuel Johnson
