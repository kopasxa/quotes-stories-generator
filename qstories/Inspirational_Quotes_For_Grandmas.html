Inspirational Quotes For Grandmas

1. “Being an everyday grandmother has kept me young at heart, I know all the newest fads and fancies. I am way hipper than most of my friends.” – Trudy Jo Hahn Snader

2. “If you are lucky enough to have your grandmother with you, sit and talk with her sometime. Ask her big questions, like what’s the secret to a happy marriage, and little ones, like when was the first time she put on lipstick.” – Erin Bried

3. “A grandmother is both a sword and a shield.” – Fredrik Backman

4. “When grandparents enter the door, discipline flies out the window.” – Ogden Nash

5. “Parent-child relationships are complex. Grandmother-grandchild relationships are simple. Grandmas are short on criticism and long on love.” – Janet Lanese

6. “There is nothing more wonderful than the love and guidance a grandparent can give his or her grandchild.” – Edward Fays

7. “Becoming a grandmother is wonderful. One moment you’re just a mother. The next you are all-wise and prehistoric.” – Pam Brown

8. “If I had known how wonderful it would be to have grandchildren, I’d have had them first.” – Lois Wyse

9. “When a child is born, so are grandmothers.” – Judith Levy

10. “Being a grandmother is the best – it can’t be overrated.” – Marty Norman

11. “One friend came up with the name ‘Glam-ma’, short for Glamorous Grandmother. I loved it!” – Carol L. Covin

12. “A grandmother once said that children are the investments and grandchildren the dividends.” – Selma Berg

13. “Truth be told, being a grandma is as close as we ever get to perfection. The ultimate warm sticky bun with plump raisins and nuts.” – Bryna Nelson Paston

14. “There are grandmothers out there who would move heaven and earth for their grandchildren.” – Janice Elliott-Howard

15. “Soon I will be an old, white-haired lady into whose lap someone places a baby, saying, ‘Smile, Grandma!’ I, who myself so recently was photographed on my grandmother’s lap.” – Liv Ullmann

16. “I want to be the grandmother who plays inside and outside, experiencing joy with each grandchild, according to his or her personality and interests.” – Sharon Carpenter

17. “A grandmother is a remarkable woman. She’s a wonderful combination of warmth and kindness, laughter and love.” – Unknown

18. “Just about the time a woman thinks her work is done, she becomes a grandmother.” – Edward H. Dreschnack

19. “Uncles and aunts, and cousins, are all very well, and fathers and mothers are not to be despised; but a grandmother, at holiday time, is worth them all.” – Fanny Fern

20. “So, what do I really know about being a grandmother? Well, I remember what I didn’t like as a child. I also loved being a Mom, but I have to tell you that being a grandmother has brought a whole new side of life – the fun side – to me.” – Janet Steele

21. “Most of us base our certainties about grandmotherhood on what our own grans were like or our own mums. So, if we don’t instantly feel we match up, it’s probably because we have a certain blueprint in our minds.” – Flic Everett

22. “Many of my friends were grandmothers and, without exception, they said, ‘Until you become a grandmother, you can’t understand how it feels.’” – Barbara LoMonaco

23. “Being a grandmother provides you with an opportunity to help your grandchild gain a sense of who he or she really is.” – Deborah Williams and Linda A. Johnson

24. “My mother and grandmother raised me. Queens raised me.” – Lamar Odom

25. “One only has to look at the vast assortment of birthday and Mother’s Day greeting cards to appreciate the depth of love some hold for their grandmothers.” – Angela Bowen

26. “Grandmothers and roses are much the same. Each are God’s masterpieces with different names.” – Unknown

27. “Every parent knows that children look at their grandparents as sources of wisdom and security.” – David Jeremiah

28. “Being a mother and grandmother, my maternal instinct made me want to do for my associates what every mother wants to do for her children – what’s best for them.” – Mary Kay

29. “If becoming a grandmother was only a matter of choice, I should advise every one of you straight away to become one.” – Hannah Whithall Smith

30. “As I learned from growing up, you don’t mess with your grandmother.” – Prince William

31. “Grandmothers are wonderful ladies who always seem to place others in front of themselves.” – Anitapoems

32. “A Grandmother thinks of her grandchildren day and night, even when they are not with her. She will always love them more than anyone would understand.” – Karen Gibbs

33. “There aren’t too many things that are more comforting on this earth than a hug from grandma.” – Anitapoems

34. “What a wonderful contribution our grandmothers and grandfathers can make if they will share some of the rich experiences and their testimonies with their children and grandchildren.” – Vaughn J. Featherstone

35. “My grandma once said that grief is the price we pay for love.” – Prince William
