Inspirational Motherhood Quotes For Daughter

1. “Having a little girl has been like following an old treasure map with the important paths torn away.” – Heather Gudenkauf

2. “We mothers are learning to mark our mothering success by our daughters’ lengthening flight.” – Letty Cottin Pogrebin

3. “Our daughters are the most precious of our treasures, the dearest possessions of our homes and the objects of our most watchful love.” – Margaret E. Sangster

4. “My mother wanted me to be her wings, to fly as she never quite had the courage to do. I love her for that. I love the fact that she wanted to give birth to her own wings.” – Erica Jong

5. “Mother-daughter disagreements were, in hindsight, basically mother stating the truth and daughter taking her own sweet time coming around.” – Barbara Delinsky

6. “A daughter is a treasure and a cause of sleeplessness.” – Ben Sirach

7. “Words are not enough to express the unconditional love that exists between a mother and a daughter.” – Caitlin Houston

8. “By the time you realize your mother was right, you have a daughter who thinks that you’re wrong.” – Sada Malhotra

9. “A daughter is God’s way of saying, ‘thought you could use a lifelong friend.’” – Author unknown

10. “No daughter and mother ever live apart, no matter what the distance between them.” – Christie Watson

11. “A mother’s treasure is her daughter.” – Catherine Pulsifer

12. “Her mother would tell her she was beautiful and that everything was all right.” – Sarah Addison Allen

13. “As they get older, our daughters become more and more like us too.” – Amy Newmark

14. “From a little girl so very small, how and when did you get so tall?” – Karen Mortensen

15. “Mother and daughter got on very well indeed, with a deep affection founded on almost complete misunderstanding.” – Mary Stewart

16. “A daughter is a miracle that never ceases to be miraculous… Full of beauty and forever beautiful… Loving and caring and truly amazing.” – Deanna Beisser

17. “No matter how old she may be, sometimes a girl just needs her mom.” – Cardinal Mermillod

18. “A mother’s and daughter’s love is never separated.” – Viola Shipman
