Best Uncle Quotes

1. “My uncle is very protective and always has my best interest at heart.” – Unknown

2. “Never was a brother or cousin – but unclehood looms, and so do lessons on wearing loud ties, talking with a full mouth, stealing a nose.” – Jeff Kleinman

3. “An amazing uncle is like a glass of refreshing lemonade in the hot summer of life.” – Unknown

4. “He is a remarkable person, and I am grateful to call him my uncle. I can’t thank him enough for being my rock and the person that I could trust to keep my secrets.” – Unknown

5. “My uncle is the combination of a second dad, big brother, and best friend.” – Unknown

6. “My uncle is from Trinidad, so, ever since I was seven, I grew up listening to Soca, the genre that’s from there. It’s my favorite sound.” – Meghan Trainor

7. “When I was young, an eccentric uncle decided to teach me how to lie. Not, he explained, because he wanted me to lie, but because he thought I should know how it’s done so I would recognize when I was being lied to.” – Brian Eno

8. “An uncle is someone special to remember with warmth, think of with pride, and cherish with love.” – Unknown

9. “My uncle was a second father to me. I spent most of my childhood with him.” – Rey Mysterio

10. “My first memory of playing music was when I was three years old in Puerto Rico. I played percussion on a tin can behind my uncle, who played the cuatro.” – Jose Feliciano

11. “Love, laughter, and care, you provided me with all that I could need when growing up. I knew that I could always count on my uncle to help me with anything that I could need. You made sure that I never felt alone.” – Unknown

12. “I will live in thy heart, die in thy lap, and be buried in thine eyes and moreover, I will go with thee to thy uncle’s.” – William Shakespeare

13. “I spent a long time away from my parents when I was younger. I would go hunting and fishing with my uncle, and we would go for weeks at a time. I also spent a lot of time in Texas with my grandparents.” – RJ Mitte

14. “Everyone needs a FUNCLE (fun + uncle). Because funcles are much more fun than parents.” – Unknown

15. “They’ve got great parents, I’m just trying to be the fun uncle.” – Sarah Silverman

16. “I think my uncle was probably the biggest influence in my life. We grew up in the same house, and he was just a really great, hard-working, honest, ethical person.” – Catherine Bell

17. “My uncle is an inspiration to me every day.” – Unknown

18. “Some people don’t believe in superheroes because they haven’t met my uncle yet.” – Unknown

19. “In my uncle, I see a friend who walks me through thick and thin and a mentor who teaches me to win.” – Unknown

21. “Watching my uncle has taught me how to be a great brother to my sister.” – Unknown
